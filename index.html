Introduction

Hello, my name is Evan McLeod, and I’m a student at the University of South Carolina Aiken pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Art. I’m also a proud member of the USC Aiken Cross Country team, and I enjoy staying active through running and working out in my free time.

Outside of academics and athletics, I’m a huge fan of superhero and sci-fi shows — especially series like X-Men, Ben 10, and Avengers. These imaginative worlds have always inspired me creatively.

My passion for combining creativity and technology drives much of my work. I specialize in 3D modeling and 3D printing, and I love designing custom action figures and sculptures using tools like Blender and Nomad Sculpt. I enjoy bringing digital creations to life through hands-on fabrication.

This portfolio is a space to showcase my skills, projects, and growth as a designer, developer, and artist.

  • 3D Printing – FDM & Resin Printing
